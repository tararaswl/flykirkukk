Our Story

Founded in 2013, Flykirkuk began as a small travel agency in Kirkuk with a simple yet powerful mission: to make travel accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

What started as a dream to connect people with the world has grown into a trusted name in the travel industry. Over the years, we've helped thousands of travelers explore destinations across the globe, from the bustling streets of Bangkok to the serene temples of Kyoto.

Our commitment to excellence and personalized service has made us the preferred choice for travelers seeking authentic experiences. Today, we continue to innovate while maintaining the personal touch that has defined us from the beginning.

Our Mission

To inspire and enable people to explore the world by providing exceptional travel services, creating memorable experiences, and building lasting connections between cultures.

Our Vision

To be the leading travel agency in the region, recognized for our innovation, reliability, and commitment to creating transformative travel experiences that enrich lives.
